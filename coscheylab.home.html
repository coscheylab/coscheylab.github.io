Coschey lab is an experimental open laboratory founded by Banan Vedro, which has a horizontal structure suitable for both a collective and residents. Coschei Lab is part of a wider community, including Hack Rhema Lab (a hackerspace in Lviv) and OSRZ (decentralized collectives of artists in Ukraine), and is engaged in recycling, working with garbage and rudiments to create various objects, installations, and products, as well as implementing cultural initiatives.
