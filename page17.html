V C F D

6hp vcf\vco\distortion

This a experimental multifunctional diode ladder filter withs distortion in the input stage, high level of resonance
and big diapason of frequency
3-5hz-40 - 50mhz ! ! !

vcfd have very sensitive controls but at the same time this allows you to squeeze out much more variety from it for your experiments.

Schematics and design made
by Banan vedro
at hack rhema lab lviv 2024

Distortion knob - 70% pre amp, 30% distortion

d.lvl is control of the amount signal after distortion stage, which directly affects on resonance intensity.

great resonance possibilities.

be careful with position after middle,
it will raise your volume when cut off is active, but clean signal will become quieter.

R.cv in and r.cv lvl work like mod for resonance.

cut off starts to work like vco when the resonance is above the middle, frequency 3-5hz-40 - 50mhz ! ! !

carefully!! the output signal can be very high, max 20Vpp, watch the volume when resonance or distortion is high

please save yours and your neighbor's ears and psyche
